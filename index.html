My Top Favorite Albums

I picked my favorite albums from 3 music artists. These artists are Laufey, Grentperez, and Rex Orange County. These 3 artists each bring their own unique flavor to pop, but they all share a penchant for smooth, engaging melodies and lyrics that range from playful to deeply introspective.These artists provide music that resonates on both a personal and universal level. It’s a great mix of emotive songwriting and lush production that’s perfect for a reflective mood or a relaxed hangout. Try and listen to their albums now!

Grentperez

When We Were Younger

is a calm, smooth, and laid-back album with soulful vibes and a nostalgic feel.

Demo(s) About Love

is a gentle, intimate collection that leans into the raw, unpolished side of his sound.

Conversations with the Moon

is a smooth, chilled-out album with a mellow, dreamy feel.

Trail Mix Tape

is a vibrant, feel-good album that blends upbeat indie pop with groovy R&B elements.

Laufey

Typical of Me

is a beautifully refined mix of jazz, indie pop, and classical influences, all centered around her elegant vocals and emotive songwriting.

Bewitched

is a mesmerizing and romantic album that effortlessly blends jazz and indie pop.

Everything I Know About Love

is a captivating, sophisticated album that blends into a beautifully emotional exploration of love, heartbreak, and vulnerability.

A Night at the Symphony

is a relaxing, melodic, and tuneful album that blends jazz, classical, and indie pop into a beautifully emotional exploration of love, heartbreak, and vulnerability.

Rex Orange County

Apricot Princess

is a warm, introspective album that blends elements of indie pop, jazz, and soul, creating a sound that’s both easy-going and emotionally raw.

Who Cares?

is an upbeat, refreshing album that blends soul, pop, and funk with Rex’s signature light-hearted, yet thoughtful songwriting.

Bcos U Will Never B Free

is a raw, introspective album that feels like a candid exploration of inner struggles and personal growth.

Pony

is a mature, emotionally charged album that blends elements of soul, pop, and alternative to create a rich and dynamic sound
